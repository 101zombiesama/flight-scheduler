How to use:

1. Set the properties in the form. Fleet size should be less the than number of airports to satisfy maximum capacity of airports which is 1

2. Use comma separated values for airports and hubs

3. Hub should be one of the airports. Maintenance of aircrafts is done at the hubs. there can be multiple hubs

4. Maintenance frequency is after what amount of time maintenance should be done

5. use 1 unit of time = 30 min

6. Create an adjecency matrix by clicking on the "Matrix" button and follow the instructions

7. Finally click the "Solve" button and wait until the calculations are done
